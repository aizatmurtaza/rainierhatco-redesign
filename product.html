About the Park

GRAND CANYON
NATIONAL PARK

One of the world's most spectacular natural wonders. The Grand Canyon stretches 277 miles long, up to 18 miles wide, and over a mile deep — its layered bands of colorful rock revealing billions of years of Earth's geological history.

Our donation funds trail upkeep on the South Rim and wildlife corridor programs protecting the park's 447 bird species and 91 mammal species.
