The Founder

IAN'S STORY

Growing up in the Pacific Northwest, Ian spent his weekends hiking the trails around Mt. Rainier. The mountains weren't just a backdrop — they were home. After years of watching budget cuts threaten park programs and trail maintenance, he wanted to do something real about it.

In 2021, he launched Rainier Hat Co. from his apartment in Seattle with a simple conviction: make a product worth buying, and donate every dollar of profit to the parks that shaped him.

Three years later, we've donated over $47,000 to the National Parks Foundation, supported 12 parks across the country, and shipped hats to hikers, climbers, and park lovers in all 50 states.

We're employee-owned and operated. No outside investors, no corporate parent. Just a small team in Seattle that loves the outdoors and wants to protect it.

"THE PARKS GAVE ME EVERYTHING.
THE LEAST I CAN DO IS GIVE SOMETHING BACK."

— Ian, Founder · ian@rainierhatco.org
